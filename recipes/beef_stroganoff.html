Beef Stroganoff

Beef Stroganoff is a Russian dish consisting of sautéed pieces of beef served in a sauce with smetana (sour cream).

Ingredients

  • Beef Sirloin or Tenderloin
  • Mushrooms
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Beef Broth
  • Sour Cream
  • Dijon Mustard
  • Butter
  • Olive Oil
  • Flour
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Fresh Parsley (for garnish)

Steps

  1. PREPARATION: Slice the beef into thin strips against the grain. Clean and slice the mushrooms. Finely chop the onions and garlic.
  2. SÉAR: In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the beef strips in batches to avoid overcrowding. Sear until browned on all sides but not fully cooked. Remove and set aside.
  3. SAUTÉ: In the same skillet, add butter and sauté the onions until translucent. Add the garlic and mushrooms, cooking until the mushrooms release their moisture and start to brown.
  4. MAKE SAUCE: Sprinkle flour over the mushroom mixture and stir well to coat. Gradually add beef broth while stirring to avoid lumps. Bring to a simmer and cook until the sauce thickens.
  5. COMBINE: Return the seared beef to the skillet. Stir in sour cream and Dijon mustard. Cook for a few more minutes until the beef is cooked through but still tender. Do not boil.
  6. SERVE: Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot over egg noodles, rice, or mashed potatoes.
