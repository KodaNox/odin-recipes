Lohikeitto

Salmon chowder is a common dish in Finland and other Nordic countries. It consists of salmon fillets, boiled potatoes, carrots and leeks.

Ingredients

  • Salmon
  • Leeks
  • Potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Fish Broth
  • Heavy Cream
  • Dill
  • Butter
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Bay Leaf
  • Lemon (optional)

Steps

  1. PREPARATION: Cut the leeks into thin strips and wash them thoroughly to remove any dirt. Peel and dice the potatoes and carrots into bite-sized pieces. Cut the salmon into large cubes. Chop the dill finely.
  2. SAUTÉ: In a large pot or saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat and sauté the leeks and carrots until they start to soften.
  3. COOK: Once the leeks and carrots have reduced in volume by about half, add the potatoes and fish stock. Season with salt and black pepper. Simmer for 15 minutes at medium heat until the potatoes are cooked.
  4. SALMON: Add the salmon cubes and cream. Cook over medium heat for 3-5 minutes until the salmon is cooked. The cream should not boil. Close the lid and wait for another five minutes.
  5. SERVE: To finish, add the fresh dill. Serve in a bowl with more dill, black pepper, and lemon to taste.
